## Recommended Enhancements

### HIGH PRIORITY

**1. Strengthen Pricing Transparency Messaging**
- PRD emphasizes this as key customer need
- Current: "No hidden fees, no surprises"
- **Suggestion**: Add to hero section or feature card:
  - "See your exact price before you book"
  - "Transparent pricing, no surge fees"
  - "Fixed rates, no surprises"

**2. Add Digital Payment/Booking Callout**
- PRD lists digital payments as core need
- **Suggestion**: Add trust indicator or feature:
  - "Secure online payment"
  - "Pay by card, Apple Pay, Google Pay"
  - Feature card: "Instant Digital Booking"

### MEDIUM PRIORITY

**3. Expand Business Account Messaging**
- PRD shows businesses as key segment
- **Suggestion**: Consider dedicated section or stronger CTA for businesses:
  - "Corporate Solutions for Local Businesses"
  - Add testimonial placeholder (future)
  - Highlight invoicing, account management, priority booking

**4. Add "How It Works" Section**
- Simple 3-step process
- Reinforces ease of use and digital-first approach
- **Suggestion**:
  1. Get instant quote online
  2. Book and pay securely
  3. Track your driver (future)

### LOW PRIORITY

**5. Consider Adding Social Proof Placeholder**
- PRD includes ratings/reviews in driver quality
- Not critical for MVP but shows quality focus
- **Suggestion**: Add section for:
  - "Trusted by Dorset Residents and Businesses"
  - Customer testimonials (future)
  - Trip counter (future)
